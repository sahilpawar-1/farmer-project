Humid Climate Crops in Maharashtra

Rice

Thrives in flooded fields and high humidity.

More Info
  • Sowing Time: June to July
  • Fertilizers: 100 kg N + 50 kg P + 50 kg K per hectare
  • Water Requirement: 1200–1500 mm total
  • Growth Duration: 120–150 days
  • Harvest Time: October to November
  • Notes: Requires standing water and warm temperatures.

Banana

Grows well in warm, humid conditions.

More Info
  • Planting Time: Year-round (best in monsoon)
  • Fertilizers: 200 g N + 60 g P + 300 g K per plant annually
  • Water Requirement: 1200–2000 mm annually
  • Growth Duration: 11–12 months
  • Harvest Time: Throughout the year
  • Notes: Sensitive to wind; needs good drainage.

Sugarcane

Needs high humidity and abundant water.

More Info
  • Planting Time: February to April
  • Fertilizers: 250 kg N + 115 kg P + 115 kg K per hectare
  • Water Requirement: 1500–2500 mm total
  • Growth Duration: 10–12 months
  • Harvest Time: December to March
  • Notes: Requires fertile soil and regular irrigation.

Cardamom

Thrives in tropical humidity and shade.

More Info
  • Planting Time: June to August
  • Fertilizers: 75 kg N + 75 kg P + 150 kg K per hectare
  • Water Requirement: 1500–2000 mm total
  • Growth Duration: 2–3 years
  • Harvest Time: October to December
  • Notes: Requires shade and well-drained loamy soil.

Black Pepper

Climber crop suited for humid tropics.

More Info
  • Planting Time: May to June
  • Fertilizers: 100 kg N + 40 kg P + 140 kg K per hectare
  • Water Requirement: 2000–2500 mm total
  • Growth Duration: 2–3 years
  • Harvest Time: December to February
  • Notes: Requires support trees and high humidity.
